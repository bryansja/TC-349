About my Idea

One of my favorite hobbies to do when I am not working is playing golf. It is a sport that I love to play. Golf is challenge and each day, even each hole my swing and results are always different. Every time you tee the ball up you never know what you are going to get. I fell in love with the sport because it is only you playing against the course and can only rely on yourself to score well. I was thinking about what I was going to do my final website on and it hit me to do a website based on golf. Each page had a different feel and information that the user could experience.
